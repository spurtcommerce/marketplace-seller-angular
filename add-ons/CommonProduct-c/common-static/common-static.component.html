Efficiently Manage Common Products Sold by Multiple Sellers

The Common Product Addon allows sellers to convert their individual products into common products that can be sold by multiple sellers. This feature helps avoid duplication and simplifies inventory management.

  • Eliminate Product Duplication : Streamline your product catalog by allowing multiple sellers to sell the same product, reducing redundancy.
  • Multiple Sellers for One Product : Each product can have various sellers associated with it, enhancing competition and options for buyers.
  • Easy Quantity and Price Updates: Sellers can update the quantity and price for common products, ensuring accurate inventory management.
  • Seller Listing on Product Detail Page : Customers can view the product details along with a list of sellers offering that product, making it easy to compare prices and options.
