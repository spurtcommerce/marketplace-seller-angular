Streamline Customer Quotes with the Request Quote Addon

Facilitate customer inquiries and pricing discussions with the Request Quote Addon, allowing customers to raise quotes that include important details.

  • Required Quantity and Approximate Budget : Customers can specify the quantity needed and their estimated budget, allowing for tailored responses.
  • Prompt Price Responses : Admins can respond quickly with pricing information, enhancing the overall customer experience.
