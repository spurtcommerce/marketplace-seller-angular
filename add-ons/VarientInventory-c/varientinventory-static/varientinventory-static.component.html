Manage Stock Levels Efficiently for Each Product Variant

Streamline your inventory management with the Variant Stock Update Addon, allowing sellers to update stock levels for each product variant individually.

  • Separate Stock Management for Variants : Sellers can specify stock levels for each variant, ensuring accurate inventory tracking even for similar products.
  • Out of Stock Threshold Notifications : Set a threshold to receive notifications when stock levels fall below a specified quantity, helping maintain inventory levels.
  • Customizable Cart Quantity Limits : Define minimum and maximum quantities allowed in the cart for each variant, enhancing customer purchasing control.
  • Back Order Management : Enable back orders and specify the quantity available for back order, allowing customers to place orders for out-of-stock variants.
